Data skills

I have gained substantial experience with dataset merging and (somewhat advanced) quantitative analysis (e.g. multilevel logistic regression). I consider myself an expert with Qualtrics and Stata, and an ongoing student of Python and R (for data visualisation). I currently assist academic professionals with various aspects of survey programming, dataset merging and preparation, as well as designing data visualizations.

I have co-taught classes on multiple regression and scale construction to bachelor students. I take pride in having a solid understanding of the basics of statistics. Please, do ask me to clearly explain the central limit theorem, degrees of freedom, or what the p-value literally means.

Data handling and rigorous statistical analysis legitimately make me happy, and I am steadily improving my quantitative skillset.
